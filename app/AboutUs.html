About us

At Destinara, we are an enthusiastic team, bring the convenience of travelling to your favourite destinations by the most scenic, and breathtaking train routes. We bring a whole to experience to passionate travellers exploring the world, by providing users with a brand new experience of travelling with the sense of playing a game.

Our objective is to give our users convenience at your fingertips, and to encourage potential users to travel having seen the experience our existing users have had with the use of Destinara.
